Three phases, one through-line

Before

Plan against your mandate

Tell Arbiter the question your board sent you to answer. You'll get a prioritized shortlist of sessions worth attending, reasoned against your organizational context rather than the conference's marketing copy.

During

Notes, the way you normally take them

Log sessions as you go. A casual check-in catches what felt uneasy or surprising, without needing you to articulate why. Arbiter tracks tension across the day and recommends what to attend next.

After

One coherent deliverable

Three priorities for your board, the contradictions you encountered and how they resolve, and talking points ready for Monday morning. You can review and edit the draft before you present it.
